Drive Controller (XB1)
	--------
	L Stick:	drive (gross control)
	R Stick:	drive (fine control)
	---OR---
	L Stick:	drive (forward/backward, strafe)
	R Stick X:	turning
	--------
	L Trigger:	reverse throttle (further it's pushed, slower you go)
Operator Controller (Logitech)
	L Stick X:	left arm movement
	L Stick Y:	left arm wheel control
	R Stick X:	right arm movement
	R Stick Y:	right arm wheel control
	+Pad X:		claw rotation
	+Pad Y:		claw elevation
	L Bumper:	claw open/close (open while held)
